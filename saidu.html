Saidu

Party games for everyone

A collection of party games you play together on one device. No sign-up, no accounts—just pass the phone and play. Perfect for game nights, trips, and get-togethers.

Games See everything included in Saidu. Privacy How Saidu handles your data.

One device, everyone plays

  • One iPhone or iPad for the whole group
  • Pass-and-play: take turns holding the device
  • No extra gear or internet needed
  • Works offline

Games included

  • Mafia — Find the mafia among the civilians. Night phases, voting, and roles (Doctor, Detective).
  • Impostor — One impostor, one secret word. Ask questions and spot the fake.
  • Alias — Describe words without saying them. Race the timer.
  • Role Call — Get a secret role and a hint. Discuss and guess who’s who.
  • Would You Rather — Pick between two options and see what others choose.
  • Most Likely To — “Who’s most likely to…?” Vote and reveal.
  • I Have Never — “I have never…” — classic drinking-game style, no drinking required.
  • Hot Seat — One person in the hot seat; everyone else asks the questions.
  • Who Is Who — Guess the person from clues and questions.

In your language

Saidu supports English, Georgian, Spanish, and German. Pick your language in Settings and play in the one you prefer.

Designed for groups

  • Clear rules and examples for each game
  • Flexible player counts (from 2 to 20 depending on the game)
  • Simple, readable interface so you spend time playing, not learning the app

Whether you’re at home, on a trip, or anywhere in between, Saidu turns one phone into a full set of party games for everyone.
