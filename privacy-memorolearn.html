Privacy Policy — MemoroLearn

MemoroLearn is designed as an offline‑first study app. Documents, notes, highlights, bookmarks, and reading progress are stored locally on your device. We don’t run our own servers for your study data, and there is no in‑app account system.

In the future, if we add optional cloud sync or analytics, this page will describe exactly what is collected, how it is used, and how to opt out. Until then, your study library lives on your device and in any backups you create.

For support or questions, see the Support page.
