Privacy Policy — MemoroLearn

MemoroLearn is an offline-first study app by NULL CLUB. This policy explains what data is used when you use the app.

1) Data stored in the app

Documents, notes, highlights, bookmarks, flashcards, and reading progress are stored locally on your device. We do not run our own cloud backend for this study data.

2) Accounts

MemoroLearn does not require creating an account to use the core reading and study features.

3) Text-to-speech

Read-aloud uses Apple system voices available on the device. Your document text is not uploaded to NULL CLUB servers for text-to-speech.

4) Ads (free tier)

The free tier may show ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may process device and ad identifiers in line with Google’s own privacy policy and your system permissions.

5) In-app purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple. Payment details are handled by Apple under App Store terms and policies.

6) Tracking transparency

If tracking permission is requested, you can allow or deny it in Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt and change your choice anytime in iOS Settings.

7) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

8) Contact

For support or privacy questions, see the Support page.
