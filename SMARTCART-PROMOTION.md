## Growth Levers

### Short-term (1-3 months)
1. **Social proof** — Get first 50 reviews on App Store
2. **Organic search** — Rank for "smart grocery list" keyword
3. **Referral** — Add share button in app (if feasible)

### Medium-term (3-6 months)
1. **Press coverage** — Pitch to tech media (MacStories, AppAdvice, etc.)
2. **Featured on App Store** — Get editorial feature (unlikely but possible)
3. **Backlinks** — Get mentions on productivity blogs, nutrition sites

### Long-term (6-12 months)
1. **Community** — Build newsletter or community for meal planners
2. **Partnerships** — Partner with recipe sites, nutrition apps
3. **Word of mouth** — Viral feature ("best meal planner for...")

## Messaging Framework

### For social media & blog posts

**Opening:** "I built..."
Example: "I built SmartCart because I got tired of decision fatigue around meal planning."

**Problem:** What pain point does it solve?
Example: "Every week I'd spend 30 minutes deciding what to eat, then another 20 minutes making a grocery list."

**Solution:** What does SmartCart do?
Example: "SmartCart lets you plan your week in 5 minutes, auto-generates an organized grocery list, and helps you track what you eat."

**Why it's different:** What makes it unique?
Example: "Everything stays on your phone — no account, no tracking, no ads. Just a calm, focused tool for eating well."

**Call to action:** Download or learn more
Example: "Give it a try: [App Store link]"
