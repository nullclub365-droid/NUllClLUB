SmartCart

Plan, shop, cook, track

Your weekly meal planner, grocery list, and food log in one place. Plan your week, shop with a smart grocery list, cook from hundreds of recipes, and log what you eat—so you spend less time figuring out what’s for dinner and more time eating well.

Get the app
How it works Plan, shop, cook, and track with SmartCart. Privacy How SmartCart handles your data.

Plan your meals

  • Set breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks for each day.
  • Use templates (e.g. high protein, calorie-conscious) or build your own week.
  • Your plan turns into a single, organized grocery list so you only buy what you need.

Shop smarter

  • Generate a grocery list from your planned meals with one tap.
  • Check off items as you shop.
  • Move checked items to your pantry so you can track what you have.

Cook with confidence

  • Browse hundreds of recipes with calories, protein, and cook time.
  • Follow step-by-step instructions and use built-in timers.
  • Save favorites in collections and get ideas for what to make next.

Track what you eat

  • Log food from ingredients (with clear portion sizes), recipes, or custom meals.
  • See today’s calories and protein on your home screen.
  • Review your food log and recipes cooked by date in History.

More to help you stay on track

  • Meal prep — Plan a batch of recipes, see suggested order and total time, and log the whole batch to today.
  • Insights — Short tips on nutrition, habits, and meal prep.
  • Backup & restore — Export or import your data so you don’t lose your plans or logs.
