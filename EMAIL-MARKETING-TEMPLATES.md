## Email Marketing Setup

### Tool: Mailchimp (Free)

1. **Create Audience:** "NULL CLUB Users"
2. **Add Custom Fields:**
   - app_name (SmartCart, Saidu, etc.)
   - last_opened (date)
   - download_date (date)
   - engagement_level (high/medium/low)

3. **Segment by App:**
   - SmartCart subscribers
   - Saidu players
   - MemoroLearn learners

4. **Automate:** Set up automation for:
   - Welcome email (day 0)
   - Power users tip (day 7)
   - Feedback survey (day 30)

### Cadence
- **SmartCart:** 1 email per week (tips, updates)
- **Saidu:** 1 email per 2 weeks (game tips, events)
- **MemoroLearn:** 1 email per month (study tips)
- **NULL CLUB:** 1 newsletter per month (all apps)

### Open Rate Targets
- Welcome: 40%+
- Power Tips: 20-25%
- Announcements: 15-20%
- Surveys: 10-15%

### Click Rate Targets
- App Store link: 5-10%
- Feature links: 3-5%
- Support/Privacy: 1-3%
