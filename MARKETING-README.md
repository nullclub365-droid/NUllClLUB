## 💡 Tips for Success

### Content Creation
- **Blog posts:** 1,500-2,000 words, 2x/month
- **Social posts:** 3x/week (consistent schedule)
- **Email:** 1x/week (Tuesday or Wednesday)
- **Video:** 3-5x/week (TikTok/Instagram)

### Timing
- Post Twitter in morning (9-10am)
- Post Reddit mid-day (10am-3pm PT)
- Send email Tuesday-Thursday
- Post social at 11am or 6pm

### Engagement
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours
- Answer questions directly
- Share user testimonials
- Celebrate community wins

### Analytics
- Review metrics every Monday morning
- Focus on 5 key metrics (not 50)
- Update strategy monthly based on data
- Celebrate wins with team

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: How long before I see results?**  
A: First traffic in week 2 (Reddit + social). Organic search (Google) takes 4-8 weeks.

**Q: How much time per week?**  
A: 10-12 hours total:
- Social media: 3.5 hours/week (30 min/day)
- Email: 1 hour/week
- Blog: 2 hours/week
- Analytics: 30 min/week
- Community engagement: 3 hours/week

**Q: Which marketing channel is most important?**  
A: Reddit + Email. These drive immediate, engaged traffic. SEO takes longer but compounds.

**Q: Do I need to hire someone?**  
A: Not for launch. Month 2-3 you might hire:
- Content writer (blog posts)
- Social media manager (scheduling)
- Community manager (Reddit + support)

**Q: What's the budget?**  
A: Free to start. Optional paid:
- Domain: $12/year
- Email: Free (Mailchimp)
- Analytics: Free (Google)
- Social: Free
- Paid ads: $100-500/month (optional, Month 2+)
