## Best Practices

### Twitter/X
- Post at 9am-10am (when people check)
- Use 2-3 hashtags max
- Include link in first tweet
- Respond to comments within 2 hours
- Retweet user testimonials

### Reddit
- Post when communities are active (8am-3pm PT)
- Be authentic (not salesy)
- Answer every comment/question
- Wait 1-2 weeks between posts in same sub
- Upvote helpful comments from others

### Instagram
- Post 3-4x per week (consistent schedule)
- Post at 11am or 6pm (peak engagement)
- Use Reels > Static images
- 10-15 hashtags (mix popular + niche)
- Reply to every comment

### TikTok
- Post 3-5x per week
- Use trending sounds (they drive views)
- First 3 seconds MUST hook
- Keep it under 30 seconds
- Film vertically

### LinkedIn
- Post 2x per week
- Share insights, not ads
- Genuine storytelling over promotion
- Include image or video
- Encourage comments ("What's your take?")
