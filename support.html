Frequently asked questions

The app is not loading or crashes. What should I do?

Restart the app, update to the latest version from the App Store, and restart your device. If the issue continues, email us with your device model and iOS version.

Where can I read your privacy policies?

Open the Privacy page and choose the app you’re using. Each app has its own policy.

How do refunds and purchases work?

Payments and refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store. You can request refunds directly through your Apple account purchase history.
