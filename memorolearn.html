MemoroLearn

Read, listen, and remember — offline

A lightweight study companion that lets you upload documents, read comfortably, listen with text‑to‑speech, and take notes — all stored locally on your device. Coming soon on the App Store.

Features What you can do with MemoroLearn. Privacy How MemoroLearn handles your data.

Read your documents offline

  • Import PDF, EPUB, and TXT files from the Files app or iCloud.
  • Scroll, zoom, and move between chapters with simple navigation.
  • Highlight, underline, and bookmark pages so you can find key parts fast.

Listen with text‑to‑speech

  • Have selected text read aloud using system text‑to‑speech.
  • Pick a voice and adjust reading speed to match how you like to listen.
  • Works offline using built‑in iOS voices — great for commuting or walking.

Take notes that stay with your books

  • Add quick text notes linked to a page or paragraph.
  • Review, edit, and delete notes from a document‑scoped list.
  • Keep everything on device — no account, no sync required.

Keep your library organized

  • See all your documents in a simple library view.
  • Track reading progress with “last page read” per document.
  • Rename or remove documents as your library grows.

Built for offline, focused study

  • All documents, notes, highlights, and bookmarks are stored locally.
  • No online account, no analytics SDKs, and no cloud backend required to study.
  • Designed as a quiet tool: minimal UI, dark‑mode friendly, and easy on the eyes.
